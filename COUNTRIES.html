In India, Food For All is a joint-venture company managed by two Indians. ... Celebrating over 12 years of leadership in food service retailing in India, Food For All now has a network of over 160 restaurants across the country, with its first restaurant launch way back in 1996.

Food For All Corporation is also available in America, founded in 1940 as a restaurant operated by Richard and Maurice, in San Bernardino, California, United States. In 1955, Ray Kroc, a businessman, joined the company as a franchise agent and proceeded to purchase the chain from the Art of Burgers.

Food For All France is the French subsidiary of the international fast food restaurant chain Art Of Burger. Its first location opened in 1972 by franchisee Raymond Dayan in Creteil, France, although the company itself still recognizes the first outlet as opening in Strasbourg in 1979.
